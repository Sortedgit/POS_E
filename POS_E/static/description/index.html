Description

Easily Send Mass Mails to all your POS Contacts.
View and manage all your POS Contacts


POSCRM Menu



Installation of this addon will display all your contacts and create a menu named POSCRM under your POS Dashboard as shown in the figure.


Select Your Contacs



You can easily select the contacts to whom you need to send emails.


Send Mails



After selection, click on Action-> Partner Mass Mailing to start sending emails to your contacts.
